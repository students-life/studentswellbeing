From online education, UUM students can free to choose the time and place. This is because the students are not constrained by pre-determined learning hours such as in the classroom or lecture. Students can choose the time to study according to their own suitability. They can work when they have the most energy, either in the morning or late at night. Students don’t have to commute to campus, so they save time and can study wherever they want. They also don’t have to get to the library because the course materials are always accessible online, therefore they save more time. Beside that, students can do flexible timetable to arrange their daily life. Students can conduct their daily activities such as study, playing and doing the exercise systematically. It can improve and maintain their self-discipline, mental and physical health. On the other hand, students can record, saved, repeat and shared the online lectures. This can allow students to access the teaching resources whenever it is convenient for them. In a word, students can learn at their own pace and study at their convenience. So that, it can improve UUM student’s wellbeing when during the online education.
