UUM Students' Wellbeing During Transition From Onsite to Online Education

During this pandemic, variety of industries, including education have been disrupted. The education system has undergone significant transformations, with many activities shifting abruptly from onsite to online. Students' wellbeing is the development and quality of life of a student include their wellness. Wellbeing encompasses all aspects of a student's life, including their mental, emotional, and physical health. Therefore, educational institutions should adapt quickly and create solutions that will enable students to attain well-being, even if they cannot relate to a model.
